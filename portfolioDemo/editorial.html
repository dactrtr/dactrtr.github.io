/ About

Ten years across visual design, UI/UX and frontend, working with distributed teams from Santiago to Tokyo, Spain and the United States. I tend to live where design meets engineering — prototyping in real code, bridging handoff between teams, and owning the interaction rather than stopping at the mockup.

Lately that means Dinopirates from Inner Space: a game universe spanning a hand-built 1-bit Playdate title and a Unity roguelike, demoed at BitSummit 2025. Constraints make interactions sharper.
