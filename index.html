I'm a UI Designer, Illustrator & Indie Game Developer who codes the things I design, from 1-bit Playdate games in Lua, SwiftUI tools and design systems.

Ten years across visual design, UI/UX and frontend, working with distributed teams from Santiago to Tokyo, Spain and the US. I tend to live where design meets engineering: prototyping in real code, simplifying handoff, and shipping interactions.

Lately that means building Dinopirates from Inner Space, a game universe spanning a hand-built Playdate title and a Unity roguelike, obsessing over how constraints make interactions better.
