### Vroom Vroom

It all started one day doing _infinity scroll_ on instagram, when I saw a video of a [guy doing tricks with a drone inside a house](https://www.instagram.com/p/Bs_-G35ni-p/). **Sweet lord**, the questions started pouring in: _how? where? when?_ I needed to know more...

My interest in getting one went on vacation when I saw the prices and how complex it was to get started knowing absolutely nothing. For those who know me, they'll know that's nothing more than a huge incentive to learn and research.

After a few articles, some price checking, and waiting for them to release an **RTF** pack — because at first the prices and all the configurations killed my motivation to _start from scratch_ — I found some people who were going to sell a **Ready To Flight** kit.

And here I am, with a **tinywhoop** trying to understand all the acronyms and above all, trying to pilot a miniature craft. It's super fun and complex, but I think it's a hobby I can handle better than the others I've tried and abandoned — maybe I should make a list of those.

Now for my **imaginary friends** who read the blog, here's what you need to get into this hobby:

## A Drone

In my case I went with a **Tinywhoop**, which is a brand of micro drones, but that's what everyone calls them. I preferred this size because I find indoor racing in enclosed spaces way more fun than flying outdoors.

A micro quad is made up of:

- Props
- Motors
- Brain
- Camera
- Frame
- Canopy

There are several parts but most of them are pretty cheap — the expensive ones are the **radio** and the **goggles**.

In a future update I'll add more info about the parts and what I've been learning, since I still haven't mastered flying well enough to start tinkering with the build (sooner or later it'll happen).

### Vroom Vroom

全ては instagram で _infinity scroll_ をしていたある日、[家の中でドローンを使って技をやっている人の動画](https://www.instagram.com/p/Bs_-G35ni-p/)を見たことから始まりました。**sweet lord**、質問が頭に押し寄せてきました。_どうやって？どこで？いつ？_ もっと知りたい...

いくつか記事を読んで、見積もりを取って、**RTF** パックが出るのを待ちました。最初は値段と設定の複雑さで _ゼロから始める_ 気が失せていたんですが、**Ready To Flight** キットを売っている人たちを見つけました。

## ドローン

自分は **Tinywhoop** を選びました。マイクロドローンのブランドですが、みんなそう呼んでいます。このサイズを選んだのは、屋外より屋内の狭い空間でレースする方がずっと楽しいと思ったからです。

マイクロ quad の構成要素：

- Props
- Motors
- Brain

- Frame
- Canopy

部品は多いですが、ほとんどは安い。高いのは **ラジオ** と **ゴーグル** です。
