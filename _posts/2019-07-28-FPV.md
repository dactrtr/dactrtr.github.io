It all started one day doing _infinity scroll_ on instagram, when I saw a video of a [guy doing tricks with a drone inside a house](https://www.instagram.com/p/Bs_-G35ni-p/). **Sweet lord**, the questions started pouring in: _how? where? when?_ I needed to know more...

## A Drone

In my case I went with a **Tinywhoop**, which is a brand of micro drones, but that's what everyone calls them. I preferred this size because I find indoor racing in enclosed spaces way more fun than flying outdoors.

A micro quad is made up of:

- Props
- Motors
- Brain
- Camera
- Frame
- Canopy

There are several parts but most of them are pretty cheap — the expensive ones are the **radio** and the **goggles**.

In a future update I'll add more info about the parts and what I've been learning, since I still haven't mastered flying well enough to start tinkering with the build (sooner or later it'll happen).

- Props
- Motors
- Brain
- Camera
- Frame
- Canopy

部品は多いですが、ほとんどは安い。高いのは **ラジオ** と **ゴーグル** です。
