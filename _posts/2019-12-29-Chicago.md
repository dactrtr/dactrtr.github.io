## Chicago

About three weeks ago, my cousin Caro ❤️ and I went to Chicago — to do what we do best: **eat**. I think this is the first time I've taken a vacation like a _normal person_, spending a lot of money on food and living a life I don't normally live the rest of the year (if you're reading this, you probably already know I'm a total _cheapskate_).

Besides taking photos with my trusty [A-6000](https://kit.co/Dactrtr/all/sony-alpha-a6000-mir) and the wonderful [Helios-44m2](https://kit.co/Dactrtr/all/helios-44-2-58mm-f2) lens, I shot some videos and played YouTube for the, what, second time? (I'll be doing another fun trip soon and I have to practice — plus my memory fails sometimes, but **the internet never does**). Not like that [Chernobyl](https://www.instagram.com/stories/highlights/17978343163085233/) trip where I never made the video, because just like that time I forgot to narrate where I was or take notes along the way, so the whole video ended up feeling like a bad dream, or a good nightmare:

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/HSQ4Fc6qnCM" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowfullscreen></iframe>

I haven't fully committed to the YouTuber life and set up a **Patreon** yet, but I do have a **kit** where I've listed some of my shameful material possessions — in case anyone believes that tools make the artist, or something like that.

I also haven't made much progress on my portfolio, but it's slowly taking shape. I've realized I'm really good at rebuilding things from scratch when I should actually be using existing elements or established rules, like [breakpoints](https://thewhitelabelagency.com/recommended-screen-resolution-for-web-design/). Silly, but there it is.

What I _have_ been doing — something I set out to do last year — is uploading almost all my drawings to [teespring](https://teespring.com/stores/dactrtr) in case anyone feels like buying a t-shirt. Too bad it won't be in time for Christmas. I'm generous though, so here's a discount code:

## Chicago

Hace como unas tres semanas atrás, fuimos con mi prima (Caro ❤️) a Chicago, ¿a qué?, a lo que somos mejores, **comer**, creo que es primera vez que me doy vacaciones como la _gente normal_, gastando mucha plata en comida y viviendo como no vive el resto del año (si están acá leyendo esto deben saber que soy una _rata_).

Además de sacar fotos con mi fiel [A-6000](https://kit.co/Dactrtr/all/sony-alpha-a6000-mir) y el maravilloso lente [Helios-44m2](https://kit.co/Dactrtr/all/helios-44-2-58mm-f2), grabe unos videos y me las di de youtuber por, ¿segunda vez?(pronto haré otro viaje entretenido y tengo que entrenar, además de que la memoria falla a veces, pero **internet jamás**), no como el viaje a [Chernobyl](https://www.instagram.com/stories/highlights/17978343163085233/) que nunca hice el video, porque como esa vez también se me olvido ir diciendo donde estaba o anotándolo, entonces todo el video al final es como un mal sueño, o una buena pesadilla:
